Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Peptide Tracker (“the App”) handles your information. Your privacy is a core design principle of the App.

Who We Are

Peptide Tracker is developed by Fouad Kassab (QUIRKY COOKING PTY LTD).

Information We Collect

Peptide Tracker does not collect any personal data.

The App does not require accounts, does not connect to servers, and does not transmit any data off your device.

Information You Enter

Any information you enter into the App (such as peptide schedules, dosages, notes, measurements, or photos) is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never uploaded to the cloud
  • Never shared with the developer or third parties

Data Sharing

Peptide Tracker does not share data with anyone. There are:

  • No third-party analytics
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No tracking technologies
  • No data brokers

Internet Connectivity

The App works fully offline and does not require an internet connection for normal operation.

Data Security

Your data remains on your device and is protected by your device’s operating system security features (such as device passcodes and encryption).

Children’s Privacy

Peptide Tracker does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of external exposure.

Changes to This Policy

If the App’s privacy practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
